About LittleNest

At LittleNest, we believe every child has a unique rhythm of growth. With the right care, patience, and encouragement, every child can reach their full potential.

We provide gentle developmental and communication support for children who experience challenges in expression, understanding, or social interaction.

"LittleNest is not a clinic — it’s a nurturing environment built on trust, positivity, and personalised care."

Our Focus Areas

Communication Enrichment

Engaging activities and guided play to help children express thoughts and emotions more confidently.

Social Skills Development

Group play, stories, and peer interaction exercises to build social comfort and empathy.

Cognitive Stimulation

Fun problem-solving games and tasks to boost attention, reasoning, and memory.

Behavioural Support

Positive reinforcement strategies to encourage focus, adaptability, and emotional balance.

Parental Guidance

Practical, experience-based guidance to help parents nurture growth at home.

Inclusive Environment

A caring, joyful space that welcomes all children and celebrates their individual pace of learning.

Our Approach

Play-Based Learning

Turning learning into fun through creative and interactive sessions.

Individual Focus

Each child’s strengths guide our methods — no one-size-fits-all.

Holistic Growth

Emotional, social, and communication skills developed together.

Empathy First

Building trust through gentle communication and a non-judgmental approach.

Meet the Founder

Priyanka Gupta

M.A. (Psychology) | Former Clinical Trainee, CRC Gorakhpur

Priyanka’s experience at the Composite Regional Centre (CRC), Gorakhpur — a Government of India institution dedicated to child rehabilitation and development — inspired her to create a space where children could grow beyond their challenges with love and encouragement.

At LittleNest, she aims to bridge developmental gaps through understanding, compassion, and structured engagement — helping each child take confident steps toward a brighter tomorrow.
